About

"Cook with Prompt" unfolded as a unique and delightful fusion of culinary expertise and creative ingenuity. Participants embraced the challenge of crafting mouthwatering dishes while navigating prompts that added an unexpected twist to their culinary creations. The event was a celebration of culinary innovation and quick thinking, where each dish became a work of art inspired by the prompts provided. From prompt-driven ingredient selections to on-the-spot recipe adjustments, participants showcased their culinary flair under the pressure of time and creativity. "Cook with Prompt" emerged not just as a cooking competition but as a platform where participants explored the boundaries of their culinary skills, transforming simple prompts into gastronomic masterpieces.
